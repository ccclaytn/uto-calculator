UTO Guidelines

UTO is given for callback work done between 12am and 6am, capped at 4.5hrs. Staff resuming work after 12 noon will not be given a meal break.

Formula: UTO = Callback duration after 12am + 1hr bonus (first activation only)

Multiple activations: Total UTO is sum of each activation, bonus +1hr only for first
